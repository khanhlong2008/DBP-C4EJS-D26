Personality

They are friendly, intelligent, and generally good with people. The Norwegian Forest cat has a lot of energy. They are very interactive cats who enjoy being part of their family environment and love to play games.
Fanciers note that these cats produce a variety of high-pitched "chirping" vocalizations
